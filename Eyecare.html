Why Healthy Eye Habits Are More Vital Than Ever

With growing screen time, pollution, and changing lifestyles, maintaining healthy eye habits is essential. Small daily practices can go a long way in preventing eye strain, preserving vision, and supporting long-term ocular health naturally.

  • ✔ Follow the 20-20-20 rule to reduce screen fatigue
  • ✔ Eat leafy greens, omega-rich fish, and vitamin A foods
  • ✔ Stay hydrated and blink often to prevent dryness
  • ✔ Wear UV-protective sunglasses when outdoors
  • ✔ Avoid rubbing eyes and wash hands frequently

Eye Surgery Procedures

LASIK Surgery

LASIK (Laser-Assisted In Situ Keratomileusis) is a popular refractive surgery that corrects vision by reshaping the cornea using an excimer laser.

  • Procedure time: 10-15 minutes per eye
  • Recovery: 24-48 hours
  • Success rate: Over 96%

Cataract Surgery

Cataract surgery involves removing the cloudy natural lens and replacing it with an artificial intraocular lens (IOL).

Traditional

Uses ultrasound to break up the cloudy lens

Laser-Assisted

More precise with femtosecond laser technology

PRK Surgery

Photorefractive Keratectomy (PRK) is an alternative to LASIK that doesn't create a corneal flap, making it suitable for patients with thin corneas.

Aspect Details
Recovery Time 3-5 days
Pain Level Moderate (first few days)
