Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our ambulance services and booking process

How quickly can I get an emergency ambulance? +

Our average response time is 8-12 minutes in urban areas. We maintain a network of strategically located ambulances across the city for rapid deployment during medical emergencies. Response time may vary based on traffic conditions and exact location.

What equipment is available in your ICU ambulances? +

Our ICU ambulances are fully equipped with ventilators, cardiac monitors (ECG), defibrillators, infusion pumps, suction apparatus, portable oxygen supply systems, emergency medications, and isolation facilities for infectious patients.

How much does ambulance service cost? +

We offer transparent pricing starting from ₹1500 for basic patient transport. ICU ambulance services range from ₹3000-₹8000 depending on distance and medical requirements. Emergency services are billed as per government guidelines. You'll receive a complete cost breakdown before confirmation.

Are your paramedics certified and trained? +

Yes, all our paramedics are certified with BLS (Basic Life Support) and ACLS (Advanced Cardiac Life Support) training from recognized institutions. They undergo regular training sessions, skill assessments, and emergency simulation drills to maintain the highest standards of emergency medical care.
