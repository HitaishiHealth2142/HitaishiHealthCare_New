🌟 Our Specializations

🩺 Internal Medicine

Comprehensive primary care for adults, managing hypertension, diabetes, and infections with a patient-centered approach.

❤ Cardiology

Expert heart care including treatment of chest pain, arrhythmias, high blood pressure, and heart failure using modern tech.

🫁 Pulmonology

Respiratory care for asthma, bronchitis, COPD, and other lung diseases with focused diagnosis and effective therapies.

🍲 Gastroenterology

Care for liver, stomach, and intestinal issues like acidity, IBS, hepatitis, and more to support your digestive well-being.

🧠 Endocrinology

Advanced care for thyroid issues, diabetes, hormonal imbalances, PCOD, and pituitary disorders using holistic methods.

🧬 Infectious Diseases

Accurate diagnosis and treatment of infections like TB, dengue, malaria, and COVID-19 with compassionate care.

🩸 Nephrology

Dedicated kidney care including dialysis counseling, chronic kidney disease, urinary disorders, and blood pressure control.

🧓 Geriatrics

Senior-focused services covering memory care, mobility support, nutrition, chronic condition management and daily assistance.

🧪 Preventive Check-ups

Health screening packages to catch diseases early, maintain long-term health, and encourage lifestyle-based wellness habits.
